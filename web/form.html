NEHRU ARTS AND SCIENCE

College

Nehru Arts and Science College (NASC) is a prominent educational institution located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Established in 1998, it offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines such as Arts, Science, Commerce, and Management. The college is known for its strong academic foundation, modern infrastructure, and commitment to fostering holistic development through extracurricular activities and research initiatives. With a focus on quality education, NASC provides students with opportunities to excel in both academics and co-curricular pursuits, preparing them for successful careers.
